Biography

I am currently a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Baidu Search. Before that, I received my Master degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Yanan Cao. My research interests broadly include Information Retrieval, Vision-Language Pre-training and Large Vision-Language Models. Now, I am focusing on the frontier exploration and practical application of Large Vision-Language Models in Information Retrieval.
