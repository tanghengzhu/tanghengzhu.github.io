Biography

I am currently a Senior Algorithm Engineer at Baidu Search. Before that, I received my Master degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS) in 2021, under the supervision of Prof. Yanan Cao. My research interests broadly include Natural Language Processing, Deep Learning and Information Retrieval. Now, I am focusing on the frontier exploration and practical application of Large-Scale Language Models in Information Retrieval.
